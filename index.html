About

PES University, located in Bangalore, India is one of the country’s leading teaching and research universities. The University is committed to providing “education for the real world” that inspires students to realize their potential.Our students graduate with the ability to adapt to an intellectually and technologically changing environment. Over the years, we have accomplished this with the participative efforts of the management, staff, students and parents. .

The Department of Computer Applications was established in 1997. Since inception, the department has been involved in equipping the undergraduates with varied backgrounds to aspire and seek career in the field of Information Technology. The result is overwhelmingly successful through Placements, Research and Entrepreneurship. PES University will introduce the first batch of its MCA graduates to the World in June 2017.

MindSpark is an annual technical event organized by the Department to tap the potentials and ignite the spark in the young minds. Technical competitions, skills development and knowledge enhancement in the state-of-the art environment is the mantra of this educational event. We welcome all undergraduate students to be part of the healthy competitions and take away bundle of knowledge and good memories.
